US vs China: A Military Comparison

The “rise” of China is based on her rapid and sustained economic growth, which includes an upgrade of China’s military power. China is not a military superpower like the United States, but after decades of increasing its military budget and deploying modern weapons systems, China can now challenge the accustomed status quo of America dictating the strategic agenda in the Asia Pacific region.
